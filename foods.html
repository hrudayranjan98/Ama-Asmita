Pakhala Bhata

📍 Famous Across Odisha

Pakhala Bhata is Odisha's most iconic summer dish made with fermented rice and water. It is often served with fried vegetables, fish, badi chura and sag.

Recipe Highlights
  • Cooked Rice
  • Water & Curd
  • Tempered Seasoning

Chhena Poda

📍 Famous In Nayagarh

Chhena Poda is one of Odisha's most iconic desserts. Made from fresh chhena, sugar and cardamom, it is baked until caramelized.

Recipe Highlights
  • Fresh Chhena
  • Sugar & Cardamom
  • Slow Baking
